Connectivity-Scalp.mat stores EEG connectivity information over the scalp model (exported from the 'Connectivity on Realistic Model' GUI).

Connectivity-Sphere.mat stores EEG connectivity information over the spherical model (exported from the 'Connectivity on Spherical Model' GUI).


Connectivity-Cortex-Source.mat stores source connectivity information over the cortex model (exported from the 'Connectivity on Cortex' GUI).


Connectivity-Cortex-ECoG.mat stores ECoG connectivity information over the cortex model (exported from the 'Connectivity on Cortex' GUI).

Connectivity-Cap.mat stores MEG connectivity information over the cap surface (exported from the 'Connectivity at MEG Sensor Level' GUI).
